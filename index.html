We are a Virtual Transport Operator

SMRT Virtual is an OMSI 2/OpenBVE simulation, based off SMRT's Trains, Light-Rail and Buses, as well as Land Transport Authority's innovations. We are in no way affiliated with SMRT, just inspired by them.

Here you'll get to see our designs and concepts, being implemented in OMSI 2 and OpenBVE. You will also get to see our ideas and products being showcased. Once again, we are not affiliated with the real-life SMRT. Currently on an indefinite hiatus, due to personal commitments.

Projects

Our Projects so far

Hillgrove Town

A map made in-house by SMRT Virtual Studios. Includes 15 drivable services – 974/A/M, 975, 976, 979, 981, 982, 983, 984, 987, 988, 989, 990 and 991.

Woodlands Town

A map made in-house by RRTS Studios, but taken over from where they have left off, by SMRT Virtual Studios. Includes 14 drivable services – 401, 858, 882, 883, 885, 889, 901, 902, 903, 911/T, 965 and IKEA Shuttle (A&S).

OpenBVE Trains

Fictional Trains modified with advanced features like STARiS etc.

Harbourvale Line Modification Project

A project to modernize the original Harbourvale Line.

Sceneryobjects (Alternative)

Fonts (Alternative)

Missing Texts in the maps? You can try and download the Developer's copy of the Fonts folder and see if the problems are solved.

Splines (Alternative)

Objects not working properly? You can try and download the Developer's copy of the Splines folder and see if the problems are solved.

ChuraKrueger (K++) Scripts

EDS not working properly? You can try and download the Developer's copy of the EDS Scripts folder for different buses and see if the problems are solved.
